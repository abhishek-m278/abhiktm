KTM ADVENTURE

The KTM Adventure is a brand of adventure bikes made by KTM two wheeler manufacturer in India. There are 2 Adv models.250 Adventure & 390 Adventure.
KTM 390 Adventure is a commuter bike available at a starting price of Rs. 3,10,883 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 2 colours. The 390 Adventure is powered by 373cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 42.3 bhp and a torque of 37 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, KTM 390 Adventure comes up with anti-locking braking system. KTM 250 Adventure is a commuter bike available at a starting price of Rs. 2,52,436 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 2 colours. The 250 Adventure is powered by 248.76cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 29.5 bhp and a torque of 24 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, KTM 250 Adventure comes up with anti-locking braking system. This 250 Adventure bike weighs 177 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 14.5 liters.
